About Us

Who we are and what drives us

Our Story

Rapid Car Rentals was founded with a single mission: make renting a car as simple and stress-free as hailing a cab. We saw how traditional rental companies buried customers in paperwork, surprise fees, and inflexible policies — and we built something better.

Today, we serve tourists, families, and business travellers across the United States and Europe, providing a wide range of vehicles — from fuel-efficient economy cars to spacious SUVs, people carriers, vans, and premium vehicles — all at transparent, competitive prices.

We are committed to providing reliable, transparent, and customer-first car rental experiences. Every vehicle in our fleet is regularly serviced, fully insured, and cleaned before handover. We back every booking with 7-day human support — not bots, not forms, just a real person you can call or email.

Rapid Car Rentals is a legitimately registered vehicle rental business operating in compliance with applicable US and EU regulations.
